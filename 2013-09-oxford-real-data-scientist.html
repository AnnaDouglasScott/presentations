Data Science in Practice

  • - What does the world want from data scientists? -- actionable insights relevant to business problems / data driven answers to significant business questions --- identify business problem, define research/analysis protocol, identify data, bring about operational change to ensure right data is collected, perform analysis, report back to 'client', close the loop on organisational change) => this is about more than data, it's about organisational transformation. -- Answers and "insight"/"intelligence" -- Basically amounts to evidence-driven everything (lean startup?)

Caveats

Training Priorities

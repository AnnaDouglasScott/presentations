A Web of Open Data

revisit the previous discussion on formats for Web data: to what extent are they discoverable and usable? (do a table of these, expanding on various criteria, including are they first class citizens of the Web? Include JSON)

a webby approach to data: RDF + URIs: context/meaning, discovery, provenance, descriptions of other data, painless data merging

RDF serialisations: pros and cons (n-triples as entry level; turtle more compact; RDF/XML the legacy choice; RDFa for embedded data; each has advantages; what about RDF/JSON and JSON-LD?)

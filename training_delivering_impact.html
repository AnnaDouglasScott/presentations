The 7 Steps

  1. Develop a compelling vision
  2. Educate, train and expand knowledge
  3. Establish a change team
  4. Be agile and plan for "quick wins"
  5. Open data needs open communication
  6. Be opportunistic, proactive and reactive
  7. Encourage, support and promote excellence

Publication phases

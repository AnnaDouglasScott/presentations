Take yourself back to 1996

Take yourself back to 1996

Move forward 18 years

no one questions the importance of the web

but web site -> web presence

The Result?

your web site is not a destination

you're in the long tail

deal with it

Where next for your Web presence?

accept the limitations of your site as a channel

...and the dominance of the major channels

analogy: are you an advertiser or a billboard operator?

Where next for your Web presence?

conclusion:

put the same resource you put into your web site
into making your content and data accessible to other channels
